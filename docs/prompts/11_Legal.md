# Prompt 11 - Legal AI Notice Simplifier

You are an AI Applications Engineer. Build the Citizen Rights module.

## Objectives
* Build legal notice scanner using Gemini 1.5 Pro to simplify legal notifications into readable summaries specifying actions and deadlines.
* Construct legal FAQ chatbot based on Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) guidelines.
* Implement contract generator (Rental agreements, name change affidavits) dynamically compiling user variables.

## Verification
* Ensure generated PDF templates comply with standard Indian stamp-paper formatting.
* Verify legal warnings and disclaimers are present before document generation.
